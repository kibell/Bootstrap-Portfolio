About Me

My name is Kitrick Bell born in one of the biggest cities in the world, Houston, Texas. Being in a city so big, surrounded by diversity, inspired me to learn about different cultures try new food, travel overseas and even learn new languages. I am inspired to learn about the world around it and everything it had to offer no matter how strange or difficult the task may be. I started off at an early age falling in love with the game of basketball. Everyday I would look forward to playing the game in the neighborhood against people of all types. Some were much bigger,older had and much more experience than I did. But I still wanted to go out there and bring what I had to offer to the court. I learned by watching how the game was played but I developed skills with hands on experiece. I continued to work on my game by practicing everyday on things that needed improvement. Working on things I personally wanted to strenghen and building on things that I have mastered and applying it in real game situations. Doing that made me a more well rounded player because I was able to be more versaltile in my position. Basketball taught me how to work with a team, and how to understand the value in what is that I have to offer. Basketball has taught me how to manage my time when it comes to handling a full schedule, and how to work extremely well under pressure.
